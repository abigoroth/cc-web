FEATURES

PURCHASE REQUEST APPROVALS

Within corporate organization, purchase will require approval from multiple authorities beforehand. Procurement.my will facilitate every party for this process.

EVALUATIONS & NEGOTIATION


Technical and Commercial committees are required to evaluates every vendor’s submissions.
To have a series of negotiations with multiple suppliers to achieve the best possible deals that can add major success to the organization through cost savings.

PURCHASE ORDER &
AWARDING PROCESS

Purchase Order can be made from Procurement.my or can be integrated with other existing system, such as SAP or any other kind & Transaction Code from email or SMS is required from top management to award most favored vendor.

REQUEST OF QUOTATIONS

Vendors from related categories will be invited simultaneously once the item list and compliance checklist are completed. Vendors are required to submit within given period of time.

ONLINE BIDDING OR NEGOTIATION

Most qualified( technically and commercially ) vendors can be invited for negotiation within procurement.my system.


ACCEPTANCE TEST CERTIFICATION

Acceptance Test Certification( ATC ) will be awarded to vendor upon completion of products or services delivery. This process may involved several parties to complete.
